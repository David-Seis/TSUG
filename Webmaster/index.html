About Us

Welcome to the Tampa Bay Microsoft Fabric Data User Group (TFAB)! We are a vibrant community of technical professionals, from beginners to experts, united by our passion for Data.

Our group offers:

  • Comprehensive Learning: Covering the entire Microsoft Fabric platform, we provide in-depth understanding of its features and capabilities.
  • Collaborative Environment: We foster a supportive and inclusive community where members can share knowledge, solve problems, and grow together.
  • Monthly Meetings: Join us in-person every third Tuesday at 6PM to connect, share insights, and learn from each other's experiences.
  • No Fees: Membership is free! There are no dues or fees to join our group.

We are proud to be associated with the Microsoft Azure Data Global Network of User Groups. Whether you're just starting out or an experienced professional, we welcome you to join us on our journey of exploring Microsoft Fabric Analytics. Welcome aboard!

Meetings

We meet on the third Tuesday of every month at 6PM. These meetings provide an opportunity to connect with fellow members, share insights, and learn from each other's experiences.

Each meeting will have introductions, a puzzler, job openings, a brief session on a useful SQL server skill or tool, a meet-and-greet, and closing.

This month's meeting will feature a great presentation by Buck Woody on "Career Paths for the Data Professional in Today's Job Market".

Make sure you RSVP on Meetup for every meeting so that we can ensure access for everyone!
